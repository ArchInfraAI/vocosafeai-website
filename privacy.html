Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 16, 2026

Our Zero-Data Policy

At VocoSafe AI, your privacy is our core principle. We built this application with a Zero-Data Architecture. This means that we do not collect, store, or transmit your voice data.

1. Local Processing (On-Device)

VocoSafe AI processes all audio recordings and speech transcriptions locally on your Mac using its built-in Apple Silicon processor. Your voice data never leaves your device. We do not send your audio or text to any cloud servers for processing.

2. What Information We Collect

We collect absolutely no personal data from the app's core functionality. We do not track what you say, what you type, or when you use the application.

The only time data is exchanged over the internet is:

  • When you manually download new AI models from within the app (connecting directly to HuggingFace repositories).
  • When verifying your subscription or lifetime purchase via the Apple App Store framework (StoreKit). This is handled entirely by Apple; we do not see or store your payment details.

3. Analytics and Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, crash reporters (like Firebase or Crashlytics), or marketing trackers inside the VocoSafe AI macOS application. Your usage remains 100% anonymous. If you choose to share crash data or usage statistics to help us improve the app, this is strictly opt-in and handled securely through Apple's official App Store analytics.

4. Contact Support

If you have any questions or encounter issues, please use our official support form. We will only use the information you provide (such as your email and error logs) to assist you with your issue.

5. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy policy, we will update this page. However, our commitment to 100% on-device processing and zero data collection will never change.
